Coffee Inventory and Product Management Project

Coffee Inventory and Product Management Project is a streamlined solution for tracking coffee stock, managing product data, and optimizing inventory operations for cafés and small businesses. Designed with an intuitive interface in Figma and implemented as a responsive website, the system offers real-time inventory monitoring, product categorization, and simplified restocking workflows — all in one user-friendly platform.
